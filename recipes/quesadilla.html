Quesadilla

Quesadillas are just like a grilled Cheese sanwchiches. All you have to change is the tortilla slices for tortillas.

Follow these steps to create the perfect grilled cheese.

  1. Add butter to your pan
  2. Butter up your toast as well
  3. Place down both slices of tortilla on the pan and wait till brown
  4. TAKE THEM OFF
  5. Add some american cheese to those pretty lil slices :) (I like to use one slice of american cheese per slice of tortilla)
  6. Put those slices together and put them on the pan
  7. Wait a little bit
  8. At this point dont let the tortilla die by burning it and take it off
  9. ENJOY
