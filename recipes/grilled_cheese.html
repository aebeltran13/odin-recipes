Grilled Cheese

Who doesn't love a goooood ol grilled cheese in the middle of the night right after loosing another match of league of legends. This is as easy as it gets.

Follow these steps to create the perfect grilled cheese.

  1. Add butter to your pan
  2. Butter up your toast as well
  3. Place down both slices of bread on the pan and wait till brown
  4. TAKE THEM OFF
  5. Add some american cheese to those pretty lil slices :) (I like to use one slice of american cheese per slice of bread)
  6. Put those slices together and put them on the pan
  7. Wait a little bit
  8. At this point dont let the bread die by burning it and take it off
  9. ENJOY
